Privacy

Project Odysseus processes learner and tutor data for tutoring operations, progress analytics, session workflows, and safety controls.

Learner account data may include name, grade, guardian contact details, login email, tutor assignments, attendance/session records, learning progress, community activity, and support or privacy request history.

Data is used only to provide tutoring services, maintain account security, support learner progress, meet operational/legal duties, and handle access, correction, deletion, or retention requests. Access is limited by role and protected through authentication, audit logging, and retention controls.

Operational cookies are used for authenticated portal access. Only safe public configuration should be embedded in client pages.

For privacy access, correction, or deletion requests, use the support channel listed in your account communications. Do not enter another person's personal information unless you are authorised to do so.
